About:

DogSoftware is an independent game and desktop application development studio dedicated to creating high-quality, innovative products for gamers and non-gamers alike. Our team is made up of passionate individuals who share a love of creating fun and engaging experiences for our users. We specialize in developing both original games and mods for existing games, as well as practical desktop applications. Our mission is to provide our users with the best possible software and gaming experiences, and we are constantly working to improve and innovate.
